Rules & SOPs

These guidelines define how SA-EMS operates in-game and out-of-character. Customize these points to match your server rules while keeping the structure for quick reference.

Core Expectations

All SA-EMS personnel are expected to act professionally, maintain medical integrity and follow both server rules and departmental policy.

  • Treat every patient with respect, regardless of scenario or faction.
  • Follow chain of command; issues go up, not out.
  • No abuse of EMS powers (reviving, transporting, or metagaming).
  • Maintain radio discipline on EMS / shared tactical channels.
  • Document calls accurately in CAD / reports as required.
